About Us

    Chowringee bazaar is a design label inspired by the varied craftsmanship from different villages across India. We strive to deliver a beautiful amalgamation of our travels with our designs. Working closely with the weavers and hand block printers, we endeavour to promote the handloom industry in our own small ways. We believe strongly in the appeal of earthy tones, beautiful silhouettes and effortless motifs. Every product has a story to tell with a lot of effort and hard work that goes behind the weaving, printing, dyeing, stitching and finishing of the product.
